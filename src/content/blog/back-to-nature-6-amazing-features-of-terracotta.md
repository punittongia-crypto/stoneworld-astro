									<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">When thoughtfully incorporated, terracotta stone blends traditional charm with contemporary style. Here are its most compelling features:</span></p><h3><b>1. Exceptional Durability</b></h3><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Despite being made from natural clay, terracotta is remarkably durable. It withstands harsh weather, resists wear and tear, and is naturally resistant to mould and bacteria. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">This makes it equally suited for outdoor applications such as </span><b>terracotta </b><a href="https://stoneworld.co.in/our-collections/natural-stone-claddings/"><b>wall cladding</b></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or garden pathways.</span></p><p><b>Pro Tip:</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> When using outdoors, ensure proper sealing to protect it from excessive moisture absorption.<br /><br /></span></p><h3><b>2. Natural Fire Resistance</b></h3><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">One of terracotta’s lesser-known advantages is its inherent fire resistance. This makes it a smart and safe choice for flooring, wall cladding, and decorative panels in areas where fire safety is a priority.<br /><br /></span></p><h3><b>3. Low-Maintenance Appeal</b></h3><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Compared to natural stones like </span><a href="https://stoneworld.co.in/blog/6-ways-to-include-marble-in-your-home-decor/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">marble</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, terracotta requires minimal upkeep. Dirt can be easily brushed away, and stains wiped clean with a damp cloth. Glazed or waterproof terracotta surfaces offer extra protection and longevity.</span></p><p><b>Maintenance Tip:</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Apply a sealant every few years to preserve its rich earthy colour.<br /><br /></span></p><h3><b>4.
